Dr. Jitendra Kumar

Dr. Jitendra Kumar joined the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli, Tamilnadu, India (An Institution of National Importance) in May, 2020. He has more than three years of diverse experience in industry and academia. Dr. Kumar obtained his doctorate degree in Machine Learning from National Institute of Technology Kurukshetra, Haryana, India in 2019 and M.Tech in Computer Science from Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra, India in 2013. He has published more the 25 research papers in reputed SCI indexed Journals and conferences. He is an active member of Cloud Computing Community, IEEE and also review board member of various journal of repute like ‘IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems’, ‘IEEE Systems Journal’, ‘Future Generation Computer Systems’, ‘IEEE Access’ etc.
